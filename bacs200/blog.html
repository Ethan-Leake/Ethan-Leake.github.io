Covid Pandemic

Social Implications

Since the beggining of this pandemic we have been quarentined and told not to go outside much. We have had to wear masks, socially distance, and wash our hands more frequently.

Economic Implications

Since the shut down many people have lost their jobs and businesses because of COVID-19 or because they are considered non essential. Also, imports have been shut down and so has travel.

Political Implications

The government has been working closely with doctors who are working on a lot on cures and all sorts of things to help get rid of the COVID-19 disease and get us back to normal. Travel bans were put up to try and help stop the spread of the disease early.
